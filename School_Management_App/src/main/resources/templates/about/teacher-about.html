Teacher Portal Overview

The Teacher portal streamlines daily instructional tasks and student progress tracking.

Main Capabilities

  • View assigned courses & today’s classes.
  • Mark & edit student attendance quickly.
  • Enter and update marks by exam type.
  • Review recent attendance and marks summaries.

Productivity Highlights

  • "Today’s Classes" widget for one‑click attendance workflow.
  • Pre‑selected schedule/date deep links.
  • Clear summaries of recent grading & attendance activity.

This page is public to help users understand teacher features before logging in.
